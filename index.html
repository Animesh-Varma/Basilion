Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 07, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications (collectively, the "Apps") developed and published by Animesh Varma (including, but not limited to, Sigil).

I am committed to building software that respects user autonomy. My philosophy is simple: Your data belongs to you. I do not collect, store, share, or transmit your personal information.

1. Zero Data Collection

The Apps operate on a "Zero-Knowledge" and "Offline-First" basis. This means:

  • No Personal Information: I do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, locations, or device identifiers.
  • No Usage Tracking: I do not track button clicks, screen views, session duration, or crash reports via remote telemetry.
  • No External Servers: The Apps do not communicate with any backend servers, APIs, or cloud storage owned by me. All processing occurs locally on your device.

2. Data Processing & Security

For Apps that involve data processing (such as encryption, file management, or note-taking):

  • Local Execution: All operations are performed exclusively by your device's hardware (CPU/RAM). Data never leaves your device.
  • Ephemeral Memory: Sensitive data (passwords, keys, plain text) is held in Random Access Memory (RAM) only while the App is active. It is cleared from memory when the operation completes or the App is closed.
  • No Persistence: The Apps do not automatically save or cache your input data to persistent storage unless you explicitly perform a "Save" or "Export" action.

3. Android Permissions

The Apps request only the minimum permissions required for their core functionality. Commonly requested permissions may include:

  • Clipboard Access: To allow you to paste content into the App or copy results out of it. The App does not monitor clipboard history.
  • Notifications: To inform you when a long-running local task is finished.
  • Storage Access: If an App supports file encryption/decryption, it may request access to read/write specific files you select.

Note: My Apps do not request the INTERNET permission. You can verify this in your device's App Info settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Because the Apps operate entirely offline, they do not integrate with third-party advertising networks, data brokers, or analytics providers (such as Google Analytics or Firebase). No data is shared with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Apps do not target or address anyone under the age of 13. Since I do not collect data, I do not knowingly hold any personal information from children.

6. Updates to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or App functionality. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the Apps implies acceptance of the updated policy.
